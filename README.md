# Facebook Advertising Campaign Analysis Report

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Facebook advertising campaigns, focusing on **impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, and audience demographics**.  
It enables marketing teams to evaluate performance efficiency and make data-driven optimization decisions.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|:--|--:|
| **Total Conversions** | 1,645 |
| **Total Clicks** | 11,674 |
| **Total Impressions** | 78,513,588 |
| **Total Spend** | \$19,620.24 |


## Visual Components

1. **Sum of Impressions by Campaign ID (Bar Chart)**  
   - Highlights which campaigns received the most exposure.  
   - Campaign **1178** leads significantly in impressions.

2. **Campaign Spend Distribution (Donut Chart)**  
   - Campaign **1178** accounts for **84.5%** of total spend, showing major investment focus.  
   - Campaigns **936 (14.8%)** and **916 (0.8%)** follow.

3. **Approved Conversions by Campaign ID (Donut Chart)**  
   - **1178:** 378 conversions (64.6%)  
   - **936:** 183 conversions (31.3%)  
   - **916:** 24 conversions (4.1%)

4. **Campaign ID by Gender (Bar Chart)**  
   - Males received more impressions and engagements than females.  
   - Indicates either better targeting or greater responsiveness from male users.


## Insights Summary

- **Campaign 1178** is the strongest performer across all KPIs — impressions, spend, and conversions.
- **Male audiences** show higher engagement and reach.
- **Campaign 916** underperforms significantly and may need re-evaluation or retirement.
- Impressions and spend are concentrated on one campaign, signaling potential optimization opportunities.


## Recommendations

1. **Optimize Budget Allocation**
   - Maintain or slightly increase funding for Campaign 1178.
   - Reassess Campaign 936’s strategy for performance improvement.
   - Consider pausing Campaign 916 until effectiveness improves.

2. **Enhance Female Engagement**
   - Rework ad creatives or messaging for better female appeal.

3. **Improve Conversion Efficiency**
   - Measure and compare **Cost per Conversion (CPC)** and **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**.
   - Adopt best-performing elements from Campaign 1178 across others.

4. **Monitor Ad Frequency**
   - Ensure impressions per user are balanced to avoid ad fatigue.

5. **Data-Driven Experimentation**
   - Replicate Campaign 1178’s targeting, ad design, and CTA success factors in new campaigns.
   - Test audience lookalikes for expansion.

## Data Source
- Facebook Ads Performance Dataset. 
- Columns: `campaign_id`, `gender`, `spent`, `impressions`, `clicks`, `approved_conversion`, `total_conversion`


### Conclusion
Campaign **1178** drives the majority of performance and should serve as the benchmark for future campaign strategies.  
To maximize ROI, focus on **balancing spend, improving underperforming campaigns, and enhancing audience segmentation**.
